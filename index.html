Controls

  • spacebar: pause/unpause rotation
  • f-F: change distance from light source
  • h: reset geometry
  • g: swap geometry

  • Autorotate (1 plane):
  • a-A: XY plane
  • s-S: YZ plane
  • d-D: XZ plane
  • q-Q: XW plane
  • w-W: YW plane
  • e-E: ZW plane

  • Autorotate (2 planes):
  • z: XY and ZW planes
  • Z: -XY and -ZW planes
  • x: -XY and ZW planes
  • X: XY and -ZW planes
  • c: YZ and XW planes
  • C: -YZ and -XW planes
  • v: -YZ and XW planes
  • V: YZ and -XW planes

  • Manual Rotation:
  • j-J: XY plane
  • k-K: YZ plane
  • l-L: XZ plane
  • u-U: XW plane
  • i-I: YW plane
  • o-O: ZW plane
